Multi-Language Understanding

Submit complaints in Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, or English. Our AI translates and classifies automatically.

🤖
Feature 2
AI Grievance Chatbot

RAG-powered assistant answers questions about complaint status, departments, and processes 24/7.

Upload a photo — our computer vision model detects potholes, garbage, broken lights, and water leakage.
